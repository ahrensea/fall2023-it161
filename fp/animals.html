ANIMALS OF LIVINGSTON CREEK: Stop by and visit our farm animals!

We want you to recall, experience and enjoy Country Living at Livingston Creek Farmers Harvest.

In addition to chickens, goats, and donkeys, you may also find the following animals here:

  • Guineas aka Guineafowl
  • Cows
  • Rabbits
  • Ducks
  • Peacocks
  • Horses

***We will even let you feed them!***
