Welcome to Livingston Creek!

OPEN DAILY FROM 9:00 AM UNTIL 6:00 PM

Founded in 2013, Livingston Creek Farmers Harvest spans 100+ acres and showcases the best of country life.

Open year-round, our slice of paradise includes a produce and seafood market full of quality products from local vendors!

Host your most special occasions at our Event Center—affectionately called Tressie's—a charming, century‐old restored farm house. Sample delicious canned jams, preserves and butters. Feed the animals and stretch your legs on the walking trail.

We are a pet-friendly facility, and offer plenty of parking and clean restrooms. ALL ARE WELCOME!

*Easy access from U.S. Route 74 highway* Located midway between Whiteville and Wilmington on HWY 74/76. Look for the red and white farmhouse!

Business Address: 49 Harvest Circle, Bolton, NC 28423
