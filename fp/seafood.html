SEAFOOD MARKET: If it comes out of the sea, we'll probably have it!

Located directly across from the produce market, the seafood market offers the freshest seafare.

Featuring all of your distant and local favorites — from crab legs to flounder to spots.

In the mood for seabass? Call ahead and we will clean the fish for you. Having a seafood boil or fish fry? We can supply as little or as much as you need!

***We keep seafood breader, hushpuppy mix, Livingston Creek cocktail sauce and other seasonings. Ask if we have shrimp shellers, fish scalers and oyster knives in stock.***
