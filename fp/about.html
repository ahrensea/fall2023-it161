Who We Are:

Livingston Creek Farmers Harvest seeks to become the rest, relaxation and premier display of all things garden.

Our location at the main corridor between Charlotte and the beaches of southeastern North Carolina provides us with the opportunity to give a truly down home farm experience.

☙ Yolanda and Danny are both native Carolinians with varied and experienced backgrounds ☙ Danny is a Marine Corps veteran and certified Master Gardener ☙ Yolanda is a former engineering manager and Clemson graduate ☙

As proud members of the North Carolina Agritourism Networking Association, GotToBeNC, and the Columbus County Chamber of Commerce & Tourism, we are privileged to offer our guests the finest products that North Carolina has to offer.

We have designed and centered our exclusive offerings around a remodeled and refurbished century‐old farm house. Come enjoy the great outdoors in — 'Our Corner of God's Country!'
