Event Center:

Looking for the perfect place to host your special event? Consider Livingston Creek's Event Center!

Affectionately known as Tressie’s, this century‐old farmhouse has been restored with character and charm.

The center features a large outdoor pavilion, spacious indoor upstairs area, restrooms and kitchen facility — all with air‐conditioned comfort!
